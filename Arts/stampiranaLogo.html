Štampirana

For a project at the university, we had to use the knowledge we gained in the course "Application of Vector Graphics in Design". We didn't have a specific task, just to do something we would like and to go through all the stages of the creative process until we make a final solution.
With my boyfriend and his brothers, we wanted to open a 3D printing shop to print models for students from the Architectural faculty
We came up with the idea of calling the company Štampirana (Print and Piranha). My task was to make a logo so students would hear about us.
I made a piranha that holds its round parts to resemble a fish, and the inside was made in a poly design because the basics of 3D modeling are polygons. We also made a website, but it's not yet functional.
