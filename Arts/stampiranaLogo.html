Štampirana

We had to use our knowledge for a project at the university which we acquired in the course Application of vector graphics in design. We didn't have a special task, just to do something that we would like to go through all the stages of the creative process until we make a final solution.
We wanted to open 3D with my boyfriend and his brothers to the printing house to print for students from Architecture model faculty.
We came up with the idea to call the company Stampirana kao pressta i piranha. My task was to create a logo so that the students heard about us.

I made a piranha that keeps its round parts like that would resemble a fish, and the interior is done in a poly design because the basics of 3D modeling are polygons. We also made site but it is not yet operational.
Software: Adobe Illustrator
Year: 2021.
