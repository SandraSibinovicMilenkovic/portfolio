Intervention

For the worksheet from the Visual Basics course, we had a task to do an intervention on an existing art work.

I have always loved the painting by Edvard Munch "The Scream" and stumbled upon the painting "Anxiety" that resembles it very much.
My first observation was that there was a gap between the woman and the man and came up with the idea to combine both images and place the figure from the "Scream" painting in the place where the gap is between the woman and man.
I dressed him in a suit to match them and intervened with color so that it doesn't stand out that he doesn't belong there. I also added more figures on the left side.


Software: Adobe Photoshop
Year: 2020.
