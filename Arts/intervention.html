Intervention

For the map of works from the subject Visual Basics, we had task to do some intervention on the existing art part.

I have always loved Edvard Munch's painting "The Scream" and I accidentally came across the picture "Anxiety" which reminds me a lot on her.
My first observation was that there was a void between woman and man and came up with the idea to combine both picture and to place the character from the picture "Scream" in the place where it is the gap between woman and man.
I dressed him in a suit to fit in with them and intervened with the color so that it does not notices that he does not belong there. I also added more characters with left side.


Software: Adobe Photoshop
