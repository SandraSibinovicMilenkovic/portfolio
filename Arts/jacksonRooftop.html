Jackson Rooftop

Logo contest for a rooftop cafe. The client did not specify where the cafe is located, nor what his target group is. The only thing he said is that the cafe is on the roof and that he would like it to be boho or turkish style.

The first thing I did was look for more boho and turkish style references and came up with the idea that I could use famous Turkish lamps instead of the letter "J" from Jackson. Decided I want the entire logo to be black so that the lamp is the only one colorful because those lamps are known for their colorful glass shades that stick together. I made asymmetrical triangles of different colors. In the end, no winner was chosen.

Software: Adobe Illustrator
Year: 2022.
