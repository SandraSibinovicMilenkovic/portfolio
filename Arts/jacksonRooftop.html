Jackson Rooftop

Contest for a logo for a rooftop café. The client has not indicated where the café is located or what its target group is, only that it is on the roof and that he would like it to be in a boho or Turkish style.

The first thing I did was to search for more references for boho and Turkish style, and came up with the idea that I could use well-known Turkish lamps instead of the letter "J" from Jackson. I decided that the whole logo would be black so that the lamp would be the only one colorful, because these lamps are known for their colorful shades of glass that are glued on. I created asymmetrical triangles of different colors. In the end, no winner was chosen.

Software: Adobe Illustrator
Year: 2022.
