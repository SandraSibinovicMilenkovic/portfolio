Publication Design

Animal Kingdom

I had a project from the Design of Publications course I will make a magazine. I called it Animal Kingdom.

It is a magazine about animals, intended for all ages who are animal lovers.

It contains interesting information that maybe you didn't know until now, stories from our zoo, like and many different kinds of animals. Interviews and advice from veterinarians as well as organizations dealing with animal rescue.

There is also a special corner for the youngest, where everyone will have fun number to find fables and interesting things related to that animal. As well as some interesting game.

At the end, there is a special section where readers can submit pictures of their pets and make them their own find the picture in one of the following numbers.

Software: Adobe InDesign and Adobe Photoshop

Year: 2022.

Groom to bloom

I had a project from the Design of Publications course I make a three-panel brochure. I chose a hair salon and dog grooming - Groom to Bloom.

The brochure provides the services that Groom to bloom provides, a map where it is, as well as the most basic things that every owner needs to know when it comes to their houses.

The brochure is humorous and cheerful design to bring smiles to animal lovers and yes they choose Groom to Bloom for their homes.

Software: Adobe InDesign and Adobe Photoshop

Year: 2022.
