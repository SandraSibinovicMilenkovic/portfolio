The spook returns

The project from the subject "Application of Raster Graphics" was to make a movie poster in the horror genre. We had to come up with the name of the movie, the actors, and the release date. We also had to use our own face and make a composite with several different images.

I chose Friday the 13th as the date because it is associated with spooky things. The title of the movie is "The Spook Returns" and a silhouette can be seen in the window.
On the poster, there are two girls who are actually one person and her reflection in the mirror. For the poster, I put on makeup using face paints that are used for Halloween makeup.
I used the fact that I have two last names and wrote Sandra Sibinović on the left and Sandra Milenković on the right, which speaks to the idea in the film that it is actually one person. I darkened the entire poster further to give it a creepy vibe.


Software: Adobe Photoshop
Year: 2020.
