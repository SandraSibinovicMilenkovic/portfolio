The spook returns

The project from the course Application of raster graphics was to let's make a movie poster in the horror genre. We should have figured it out the name of the movie, the actors and the release date of the movie. Too we should have used our face and made a composite with more different pictures.

I chose the date Friday the 13th because that date is connected with spooky things. The title of the film is The spook returns and on a silhouette can be seen in the window.
There are two girls on the poster who are actually one person and her reflection in the mirror. For the purposes of the poster, I put on make-up face paints used for Halloween make-up.
I took advantage of the fact that I have two surnames on the left side as well written by Sandra Sibinović, and from the right Sandra Milenković what it speaks to the very idea of the film that it is actually one person. Whole I additionally darkened the poster to give it a scary tone.


Software: Adobe Photoshop
Year: 2020.
