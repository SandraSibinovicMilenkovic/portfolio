Plumbing “n” things

Contest for a logo for a plumbing company - Plumbing "n" Things.

The client had no requests or told us anything so we would at least know what he would like. This gave me the freedom to be creative and make this logo. I made the word "Plumbing" from pipes and water drips from the letter "g". Also water wrench on the letter "i". For "n" things, I wrote it using an existing font, but I made the quotation marks also out of pipes.

Software: Adobe Illustrator
Year: 2022.
