Plumbing “n” things

Plumbing "n" logo contest things.

The client did not have any requirements, nor did he say anything for us to know what he would like. This gave me the freedom to play around and create this logo. I made the word "Plumbing" out of pipes, so that water flows out of the letter "g", and on the letter "i" there is a water wrench. For "n" things, I wrote it with an existing font, but I made the quotation marks out of pipes as well.

Software: Adobe Illustrator
Year: 2022.
