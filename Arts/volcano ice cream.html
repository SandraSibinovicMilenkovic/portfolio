Volcano ice cream

The project for the "Applications of Raster Graphics" class was to make an advertisement for a product that was randomly assigned to us. I got the ice cream product "Vulkan Raspberry-Vanilla."

We had to include the company's logo, slogan, and a solution that would fit the theme.
The slogan was "A New Eruption of Taste," so I came up with the idea of making a vanilla volcano that erupts raspberry lava. I chose a font that looked like melting ice cream.
I received a compliment from my professor, who said that this solution was one of the most original advertisement solutions she has seen so far.


Software: Adobe Photoshop
Year: 2020.
