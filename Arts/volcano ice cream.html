Volcano ice cream

The project from the course Application of raster graphics was to Let's make an advertisement on a topic that we like Western. I got Vulcano raspberry-vanilla ice cream.

We had to put the logo of the company whose product, slogan and some solution that will match the topic we have been given.
The slogan is "A new eruption of taste" so I came up with the idea to I make a vanilla volcano that spews raspberry lava. Choose the very font that looks like melting ice cream.
I received a compliment from the professor that this is the solution, until now, one of the most original advertising solutions by in her opinion.


Software: Adobe Photoshop
Year: 2020.
