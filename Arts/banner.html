Advanced course Adobe Illustrator

Project from the "Applications of Raster Graphics" subject was to create a banner in three different sizes so that it always has the same elements.
The theme was a banner for an advanced Adobe Illustrator course. The conditions were that the banner contains a logo, course title, duration, action button, website link, and that we use at least two images and create our own solution.

Also, it was necessary to decide which of the three banners we will convert into a gif.
