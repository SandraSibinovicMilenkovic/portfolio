Monster

We had a project to make from the Visual Effects course video in After Effects.
I called him Monster, and it's about a little boy who dreams of monsters. I was drawing worked on illustrations in Adobe Illustrator and then in After Effects the animation itself.
In Premiere, I inserted the sound that I am further refined to fit the events of the film.

Year: 2022.

Clown

From the subject Praktikum 3D modeling and animation had we are tasked to create a 20 second animation with the given clown model.
We had to come up with a scenography and yes in the first two seconds the clown jumped out of the box and was obliged a close-up shot where we had to do some vocalization the sentences we come up with.
My idea was that the ball breaks the window and wake up the clown. He got angry and said, "Who is bothering me? rest" and clicks the secret self-destruct button behind the shelf, which awakens another devilish clown. The first clown actually was devil box cover.

Software: Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe Illustrator
Year: 2021.
