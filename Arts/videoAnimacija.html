Monster

I called him Monster, and it's about a little boy who dreams of monsters. I called it "Monster" and it's about a little boy who dreams of monsters. I drew the illustrations in Adobe Illustrator and then in After Effects I created the animation.

Clown

For the Practicum 3D Modeling and Animation course, we had the task of creating a 20-second animation with the given model of a clown.
We had to create a scenography and in the first two seconds the clown had to jump out of the box. Also, we had to have one close-up shot where we had to do the vocalization of a sentence that we came up with.
My idea was that a ball breaks the window and wakes up the clown. He gets angry, says "Who is disturbing my peace," and clicks the secret self-destruct button behind the shelf, which wakes up another devilish clown. The first clown was actually the lid of the devil's box

Software: Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe Illustrator
Year: 2021.
