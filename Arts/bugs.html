World of bugs

Logo contest for bug lovers blog - World of bugs. The client asked for the letter "O" to replace the Earth and would like to the bug is incorporated into typography.

I searched the internet for interesting types of bugs that could to attract attention and came across colorful and beautiful bugs for which I didn't know they even existed. I tried in Illustrator to draw a bug in my own way so that it seems likeable and not at all scary and to respect the wishes of the client.

Software: Adobe Illustrator
Year: 2022.
