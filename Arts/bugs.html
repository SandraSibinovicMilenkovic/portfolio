World of bugs

Contest for a logo for a bug lover's blog - World of Bugs. The client requested that the letter "O" be replaced by Earth and that he would like the bug, to be incorporated into the typography.

I searched the internet for interesting species of bugs that could attract attention, and came across colorful and beautiful bugs that I didn't know existed. In Illustrator, I tried to draw the bug in my own way so that it looks appealing and not scary at all and to fulfill the client's wishes.

Software: Adobe Illustrator
Year: 2022.
