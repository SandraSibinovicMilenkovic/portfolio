Corporate Identity

Bonsai landscape

In the Corporate Identity course, we had a task to choose a company from the given options and create a name, a complete corporate identity, and then place it in a graphic standards book.

I chose the theme of bonsai and named the company " Bonsai landscape" because there is a legend that bonsai originated in China as a miniature landscape for the emperor at the time.

I chose a logo written in Great Vibes Regular font with modifications to the stroke by adding lines to the letters "z" and "Å¾", and a letter "p" that has an addition of a large letter "P" from the original font.

On the letter "i", I erased the dot and made it grow into a tree because the bonsai tree is known for being able to grow in the most unusual places.

Software: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop

Year: 2022.
