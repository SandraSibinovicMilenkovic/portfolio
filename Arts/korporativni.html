Corporate Identity

Bonsai landscape

From the subject Corporate identity, we had the task to let's choose a company from those offered and come up with a name, the whole thing corporate identity and then place it in the graphic book standards.

I chose the topic of bonsai and named the company Bonsai Landscape because there is a legend that bonsai originated in China as a miniature landscape of the then emperor.

I decided on a logo in Great Vibes Regular letter with changes in the stroke by adding dashes to the letters "z" and "Å¾" as well as the letter "p" which has the addition of a capital letter "P" from original letter.

I erased a dot on the letter "i" and made it grow out of it tree because the bonsai tree is known for what it can do it grows in the strangest places.

Software: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop

Year: 2022.
